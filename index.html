CS1Day

Join us on Thursday, Novermber 4th 2021, for a hackathon dedicated to first year Computer Science students.

After noticing an uptick in new computer science students, teachers Tom Swope and Justin Mansor, along with the computer science club, decided to create an event to inspire CompSci 1 and AP Computer Science Principles students to continue their journey in computer science education. The event will feature a drone obstacle course, a coding competition, and various speakers from the computer science field.
